    The artical

    Born in the 80s in Valencia, Spain, Coté Escrivá is a renowned artist that mixes Illustration, graphic design and sculpture in his work with influences from pop culture and animation, which remind us of our childhood, but with a sinister touch that brings you closer to the Lowbrow style. He has worked with many international brands and his work has been exhibited in London, Barcelona, ​​Los Angeles and Singapore. An illustrator whose name sounds everywhere, and now will also conquer the NFT space and the Metaverse.
